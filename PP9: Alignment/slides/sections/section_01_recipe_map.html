The Post-Training Map

Every aligned LLM walks this pipeline.

\[\max_{\htmlClass{k-dim}{\pi_\theta}} \mathbb{E}\!\left[\htmlClass{k-dim}{r_\theta(x,y)}\right] - \htmlClass{k-dim}{\beta\, D_{\mathrm{KL}}\!\left(\pi_\theta \;\|\; \pi_{\mathrm{ref}}\right)}\]
\[\max_{\htmlClass{k-policy}{\pi_\theta}} \mathbb{E}\!\left[\htmlClass{k-dim}{r_\theta(x,y)}\right] - \htmlClass{k-dim}{\beta\, D_{\mathrm{KL}}\!\left(\htmlClass{k-policy}{\pi_\theta} \;\|\; \htmlClass{k-dim}{\pi_{\mathrm{ref}}}\right)}\]
\[\max_{\htmlClass{k-dim}{\pi_\theta}} \mathbb{E}\!\left[\htmlClass{k-reward}{r_\theta(x,y)}\right] - \htmlClass{k-dim}{\beta\, D_{\mathrm{KL}}\!\left(\pi_\theta \;\|\; \pi_{\mathrm{ref}}\right)}\]
\[\max_{\htmlClass{k-policy}{\pi_\theta}} \mathbb{E}\!\left[\htmlClass{k-reward}{r_\theta(x,y)}\right] - \htmlClass{k-kl}{\beta\, D_{\mathrm{KL}}\!\left(\htmlClass{k-policy}{\pi_\theta} \;\|\; \htmlClass{k-ref}{\pi_{\mathrm{ref}}}\right)}\]
πθ — the policy we are training: a language model mapping prompt → completion.
rθ(x,y) — scalar reward from the Reward Model; higher = more preferred response.
β·KL(π‖πref) — KL penalty keeping πθ near the SFT reference; prevents reward hacking.
